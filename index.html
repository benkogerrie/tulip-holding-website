Local partner for Czech real estate

Tulip Holding s.r.o. combines hands-on asset management with an investor mindset. We manage and optimise commercial assets in the Czech Republic for institutional and professional owners – and selectively co-invest where interests are aligned.

Asset manager first, investor by design

We are not a passive property manager. Tulip acts as an asset manager with clear value-creation plans and, where appropriate, as a co-investor alongside the owner.

Institutional-grade asset management

Rent optimisation, lease restructuring, vacancy reduction and cashflow control – reported in a way institutional investors expect.

Aligned co-investor

In selected situations Tulip co-invests, aligning interests and supporting capex, repositioning and future exit.

Focused on Czech Republic

Deep familiarity with Czech tenants, legal and tax frameworks, municipalities and transaction practice.
