About us

Established in 2007, TULIP Holding builds on the extensive experience of its partners and management, who have been actively involved in real estate investment and development in the Czech Republic since 1995.

Our core business focuses on investments in income-generating properties and real estate development, with a primary presence in Brno, Prague and Berlin. Our investment activities are primarily centered on commercial, multi-purpose properties such as office buildings, industrial sites, and logistics facilities.

With a highly professional and experienced management team, supported by a network of expert advisors and stable financial backing, TULIP Holding excels at acquiring income-generating properties, managing their operations, and facilitating their eventual sale.

We are dedicated to delivering high-quality solutions that meet the evolving needs of the commercial and industrial real estate market.
